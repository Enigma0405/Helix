# Helix Demo Script

**Platform:** Helix
**Tenant:** Aetheris BioPharma Private Limited
**Tagline:** Evidence before AI. Always.
**Estimated Time:** 4 Minutes

---

## 1. Introduction (0:00 - 0:30)

* **Visual:** Open to Landing Page / Login Screen.
* **Talk Track:**
  "Welcome to Helix. Helix is an enterprise EvidenceOps platform built specifically for highly regulated industries like biopharma manufacturing. Our core philosophy is simple: Evidence before AI. Always.
  
  Today, we are operating within the tenant environment of Aetheris BioPharma Private Limited. I'll log in as the Quality Assurance Director to show you how we've transformed the deviation management process."

## 2. Problem Statement & Mission Control (0:30 - 1:15)

* **Visual:** Click Login. Arrive at Mission Control Dashboard.
* **Talk Track:**
  "In traditional manufacturing, when a quality event happens—like a sensor drifting or an out-of-spec test—Quality Assurance teams spend weeks manually piecing together batch records, historian data, and SOPs from scattered silos.
  
  Here in Mission Control, Helix separates signal from noise. We aren't looking at generic tasks; we are looking at real manufacturing deviations mapped against Aetheris BioPharma’s live organizational knowledge graph. Let's open this recent major signal regarding a particulate sensor."

* **Visual:** Click on the first active investigation in the queue.

## 3. Evidence Workspace (1:15 - 2:00)

* **Visual:** Arrive at the Investigation Detail page. Point out the three columns.
* **Talk Track:**
  "Traditional QMS software gives you a blank text box. Helix gives you an EvidenceOps Workspace.
  
  On the left, we have the Evidence Dropzone where operators upload logs, batch records, and photos. 
  
  On the right, Helix automatically binds this investigation to the exact Equipment, Personnel, and SOPs involved using our Organization Memory architecture. It intelligently traces the anomaly directly to the facility floor."

## 4. Assessment & AI Reasoning (2:00 - 3:00)

* **Visual:** Click "Upload" on the left. Then click "Run Assessment" in the center. Wait for the Assessment to render.
* **Talk Track:**
  "Watch what happens when we run the Intelligence Layer. Helix isn't a chatbot. It runs deterministic reasoning powered by Fireworks AI and AMD Instinct MI300X accelerators. 
  
  It reads the uploaded evidence, cross-references it with Aetheris’s historical knowledge on the right, and generates an Evidence-Backed Assessment. 
  
  Notice how it explicitly maps Observed Facts, identifies Evidence Gaps, and calculates confidence intervals. We are achieving sub-second reasoning over dense manufacturing parameters, completely eliminating the manual RCA grunt work."

## 5. CAPA & Human Verification (3:00 - 3:45)

* **Visual:** Scroll down to the Strategic CAPA Workflow.
* **Talk Track:**
  "But in a regulated industry, AI does not make the final call—humans do. Models observe. Systems decide. Humans remain accountable.
  
  Helix auto-drafts a 3-step Corrective and Preventive Action (CAPA). It identifies the Root Cause, recommends Immediate Containment, and suggests a Systemic Preventive Action. Every step links back to the original evidence."

* **Visual:** Click "Approve & Close Investigation".
* **Talk Track:**
  "As the QA Lead, I review the reasoning, verify the facts, and click Approve. An investigation process that used to take 3 weeks is now completed in 3 minutes."

## 6. Closing (3:45 - 4:00)

* **Visual:** Return to Dashboard.
* **Talk Track:**
  "This is the future of industrial quality management. Helix transforms organizational knowledge into evidence-backed operational intelligence. Thank you."
