# Future Roadmap

Helix is designed to evolve from an investigative co-pilot into a fully autonomous, predictive intelligence layer for global manufacturing.

## Current: MVP (Hackathon Release)
**Focus: Reactive Investigation & CAPA Drafting**
- Organization Memory graph (Equipment, Personnel, SOPs, Batches).
- Deterministic Evidence Tracing.
- Structured AI generation of Root Cause and CAPA via Fireworks AI.
- Enterprise-grade EvidenceOps UI.

## Phase 1: Integration & Automation (Months 1-6)
**Focus: Removing the Human Bottleneck in Data Gathering**
- **SCADA / Historian Integration:** Direct API hooks into OSIsoft PI and factory SCADA systems to automatically attach telemetry data to investigations.
- **ERP Integration:** Syncing batch records and supplier data from SAP/Oracle natively.
- **Continuous Compliance:** Real-time checking of deviation trends against FDA/EMA alert thresholds.

## Phase 2: Predictive Intelligence (Months 6-12)
**Focus: Proactive Quality Assurance**
- **Anomaly Forecasting:** Analyzing micro-drifts in calibration data across thousands of assets to predict failures before they trigger an official deviation.
- **Supplier Risk Graph:** Automatically flagging risky raw material lots based on historical deviation patterns across the industry.
- **Multi-Tenant Federation:** Allowing large enterprises to securely share anonymized anomaly signatures across their global network of facilities.

## Phase 3: The Standard for EvidenceOps (Year 2+)
**Focus: Autonomous Verification**
- **AI-Driven Release (Review by Exception):** Helix autonomously releases batches that have zero anomalies and perfectly matching evidence, requiring human intervention *only* when the AI flags a gap.
- **Domain Packs:** Expanding the Organization Memory schema from Biopharma into Aerospace, Semiconductor, and Automotive manufacturing.
