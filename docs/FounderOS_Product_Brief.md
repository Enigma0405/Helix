## 1. Executive Summary

Project Helix is the pioneer of the **EvidenceOps** category—a novel methodology and software platform designed to manage the lifecycle of quality evidence in highly regulated, high-stakes manufacturing environments (Pharmaceuticals, Medical Devices, Aerospace, and Bioprocesses).

Instead of treating quality incidents as administrative tick-box exercises or building generic AI text-wrappers, Helix introduces a closed-loop system where unstructured logs, telemetry, standard operating procedures (SOPs), and batch records are converted into verifiable, cited root-cause hypotheses. The system ensures that **AI acts as a copilot, humans remain the sole decision-makers, and every decision loop feeds back into a searchable, compounding organizational intelligence layer.**

---

## 2. The Problem: Quality Investigations Are Broken

In regulated manufacturing, a single batch sterility failure or component deviation can cost between **$50,000 and $2,000,000** in lost product, delays, and compliance citations. 

Today's investigation process is crippled by three core issues:
1. **The Unstructured Evidence Avalanche**: Quality engineers spend up to 70% of their investigation time manually digging through scattered documents—SOPs, raw sensor logs, calibration records, batch sheets, and environmental monitoring reports.
2. **The Compliance-Hallucination Conflict**: While generic generative AI (like ChatGPT) can summarize files, they cannot be trusted in clinical, aerospace, or pharmaceutical settings because they hallucinate facts and lack source-level auditability.
3. **The Amnesia Loop**: Organizations repeatedly run the same root-cause investigations. Lessons from a deviation on Line 3 in 2024 are rarely captured or made searchable for an engineer investigating a similar deviation on Line 1 in 2026.

---

## 3. The Solution: The EvidenceOps Platform

Helix introduces **EvidenceOps**, organizing quality control around a structured, 5-stage value loop:

```
    [ Evidence Ingestion ]
             │ (Multi-format parsers: PDF, CSV, DOCX)
             ▼
    [ Understanding & Search ]
             │ (Local pgvector semantic search & Sop/Batch context extraction)
             ▼
    [ AI Assistance ]
             │ (Adapter-neutral LLM generation of ranked hypotheses)
             ▼
    [ Human Decision ]
             │ (Analyst reviews grounded citations & edits corrective actions)
             ▼
    [ Knowledge Capture ]
             │ (Approval triggers closed-loop organizational learning)
```

### Core Product Tenets:
*   **AI Suggests, Humans Decide**: The AI prepares evidence-backed recommendations and drafts Corrective and Preventive Actions (CAPA). Humans remain strictly accountable and authorize every action.
*   **100% Citation Grounding**: Every hypothesis generated by the platform is tied to direct, highlighted substring references inside the ingested evidence files. If the AI cannot cite it, it cannot say it.
*   **Compounding Memory**: When a CAPA plan is approved, the system automatically extracts the root-cause fingerprint and registers it in the organizational knowledge layer, allowing future incidents to detect similarities in real time.

## 4. Market Opportunity & Target Segments

The Global Quality Management Software (QMS) market is projected to reach **$16.6 Billion by 2030**, growing at a CAGR of 9.7%. Helix targets the high-value compliance-critical segments where operational downtime and safety deviations carry severe regulatory penalties:

1. **Biotech & Pharmaceuticals (Primary)**: Enforcing strict FDA 21 CFR Part 11 and GMP (Good Manufacturing Practices) guidelines.
2. **Medical Device Manufacturers**: Compliance with ISO 13485 and FDA 510(k) approval cycles.
3. **Aerospace & Defence**: ISO 9001 and AS9100 quality traceability requirements.
4. **Precision Chemical & Semiconductor Fab**: High-frequency environmental and chemical calibration loops.

## 5. Business & Commercialization Model

Helix operates on a hybrid B2B enterprise model optimized for landing-and-expanding within manufacturing facilities:

*   **Core Subscription (SaaS or Private Cloud)**: Tiered seat licensing based on users (Analysts, Managers, Admins).
*   **Usage-Based Ingest**: Tiered pricing based on volume of evidence parsed, stored, and vectorized (GB or Page-count bounds).
*   **Inference Compute Passthrough**: Predictable API compute credits linked to the Fireworks AI / AMD GPU workloads, or local flat-rate licensing for on-premises deployments.

## 6. The FounderOS Alignment

Helix serves as a case study for the **FounderOS** playbook—the modern paradigm for building high-leverage, capital-efficient, and AI-native startups:
*   **Hardware & Provider Independence**: By building pluggable adapters, Helix avoids vendor lock-in. We can swap LLM APIs or host local weights without altering business layers, preserving pricing power.
*   **Building in Public & Developer-Centric**: We expose health, benchmarking, and evaluation frameworks directly in the product repository, reducing the sales cycle via engineering credibility.
*   **High-Leverage Engineering**: Using agentic developer tooling and modular architecture, a small technical team can compete with legacy enterprise QMS incumbents.
