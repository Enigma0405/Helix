# Helix: Demo Script (5 Minutes)

**Speaker Instructions:** 
- Maintain a professional, enterprise tone. 
- Do not rush. Let the UI animations speak for themselves.
- Speak definitively. No "umms" or "maybes".

---

## 0:00 - The Problem
"Good afternoon. We are presenting Helix. In regulated industries like biopharma and manufacturing, the cost of a mistake isn't just financial—it's human. Yet today, quality management systems are passive databases. When a deviation occurs, QA engineers spend weeks manually correlating evidence against tens of thousands of SOPs, batch records, and historical investigations."

## 0:45 - The Solution & Landing
"This manual process is reactive, slow, and error-prone. Enter Helix. Helix is an AI-native Enterprise Operational Intelligence platform built on a philosophy we call EvidenceOps: Models observe, systems decide, and humans remain accountable. We never let AI guess. We force it to verify."

## 1:15 - Mission Control (Dashboard)
*(Log into the platform using demo@helix.ai)*
"Welcome to Mission Control for our demo tenant, Aetheris BioPharma. Unlike a traditional static dashboard, this is a live operations pipeline. On the left, we have our canonical Organization Memory—the ground truth of all approved procedures and rules. In the center, we monitor live operational events."

## 2:00 - Operational Events & Ingestion
*(Click the Upload Zone and select the dummy LIMS report)*
"Let's simulate a live event. We ingest an Environmental Monitoring report from the cleanroom. Watch the AI Verification terminal. Helix doesn't just read the document; it is instantly cross-verifying the data against Aetheris BioPharma's specific SOPs and historical memory. In seconds, it detects a limit exceedance, correlates it with a similar historical CAPA, and automatically escalates an Operational Signal."

## 2:45 - Investigation & Evidence
*(Click the newly generated Operational Signal to enter the Investigation Detail Page)*
"Because the signal breached safety thresholds, Helix automatically opened this Investigation Workspace. Here, we see the raw evidence on the left. But look at the right sidebar: Intelligent Tracing. Helix has already mapped the exact employee, the specific sterile filter involved, and the exact violated SOP (SOP-STER-014). Let's view the Master Record."
*(Click View Master Record)*
"Every action is logged in an immutable ledger. Complete explainability."

## 3:30 - Assessment & CAPA
"Because the evidence is fully correlated, the AI reasoning engine has already executed an autonomous assessment. It isolated the root cause: a failure to adhere to the 5-minute pre-wetting requirement. Based on this deterministic trace, it has drafted a Corrective and Preventive Action (CAPA) plan for human review."
*(Click Approve & Close)*
"The QA Lead simply verifies the trace, approves the CAPA, and the system archives this learning back into the Organization Memory. The loop is closed."

## 4:15 - Architecture & AMD
"How is this possible in real-time? Helix relies on rigorous structured data extraction and rapid vector correlation. This requires massive parallel compute. We partnered with Fireworks AI, utilizing their infrastructure powered exclusively by AMD Instinct GPUs, to achieve the sub-second inference latency required for live operational monitoring."

## 4:45 - Closing
"Helix is not a chatbot. It is a deterministic, enterprise operating system for regulated workflows. Evidence before AI. Always. Thank you."
